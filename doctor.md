# Doctor


### Process:
Need to be enrolled at a university and a professor is assigned to you.
The professor must guide/support you in your thesis topic.

### Sponsorship
You either need to get the position that is already paid by the company or
You managed to get the sponsorship through various grants inside/outside the academic region or 
You support yourself

### How the application process looks like
Either follow the reserarch proposal from the universtiy/institute that you are working for or
Provide your own research proposal for the work or

### Duration
Usually last betweeen 2-3 years for a full-time role
For part-time role have seen on LinkedIn that it could last as long as 7 years and advantage is you get to do this while working at your full-time job.

Highly specialized field and the opportunity to make huge impact on the IT field through high-quality research..
